Place your logo files here. The system uses these files:

  logo.svg  or  logo.png   —  Full logo (login page, large display)
  emblem.svg  or  emblem.png  —  Small icon (sidebar, 36x36)
  favicon.svg  or  favicon.png  —  Browser tab icon (32x32)

Drop your .png or .svg file, rename it to one of the names above,
and the system will pick it up automatically.
